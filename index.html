To get custom art, sign up on Patreon at $30 or more. Wait a couple months and just DM me your idea, I'll simply draw it. Think of it like a piggy bank. All your money spent on Patreon is your allowance to spend on commissions. For each month pledged, you get tokens. Tokens get your custom art. 4 tokens for a colored sketch, 8 for an HD pic. Tokens will always be honored - even if I close Patreon and 10 years pass, I'll draw your custom if you have unspent tokens. This slow system allows me to always be open to comms and accept everyone's ideas, so I can draw for everyone!
